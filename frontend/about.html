About Scrollix

India ka Hinglish digital magazine — viral news, student life, AI tools aur earning tips. Daily fresh, always honest.

Humara Story

Scrollix ek student ne shuru kiya — ek simple idea ke saath: India mein kaafi students aur young professionals hain jinhe quality content chahiye apni language mein. Na zyada formal English, na sirf Hindi — balki ek mix jo real lagta ho.

Hum daily publish karte hain viral news, tech reviews, AI tool guides, aur practical earning tips. Har cheez jo hum likhte hain, wo genuine research aur real experience pe based hoti hai.

Scrollix free hai, aur hamesha free rahega. Hum Google AdSense aur affiliate links ke zariye earn karte hain — aur har affiliate link clearly disclosed hoti hai.
